CSS3 Gradient 分为线性渐变(linear)和径向渐变(radial)
        linear-gradient(to left, red, orange,yellow,green,blue,indigo,violet)
        参数1: 方向，默认'180deg'，等同于'to bottom'
            0deg   - to top
            90deg  - to right
            180deg - to bottom
            270deg - to left
                   - to top left
                   - to top right
        参数2,3...: 颜色的起点和终点，可以有多个颜色值
